project name

Bloom Agencies is an independent architectural product and contract agency group that represents high quality, high design contract furniture manufacturers. Bloom is about providing exceptional service to the design, architectural and contract dealer community with the same service passion as our manufacturing partners. We are committed to finding the right product solutions to you and your clients.

Designer, art director and hand lettering artist David Milan places his art center stage. By including only the most crucial elements on the header and opting for a simple monochrome palette, David draws visitors’ eyes directly to his colorful designs. His gallery of works spans the width of the screen, making up the majority of the website. David has used the Wix Pro Gallery to form a feed-like website layout that visitors are invited to scroll down for great usability. This straightforward structure allows for more and more images to be uploaded, making for a design portfolio that is easy to update as his body of work expands..
